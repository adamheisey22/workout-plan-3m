Program Notes
📈 Weight progression: Add 5 lb to DB lifts when you hit the top rep target for all sets. Add 5–10 lb to squat/deadlift each month.
💪 Push-ups for size: Since you can do 25+ easily, we're using harder variations (archer, decline, weighted) and slow tempos (3s down) to keep the muscle under tension long enough to grow.
🧗 Climbing = back day. Your rows and climbing together hit lats, rhomboids, and biceps — no extra pull day needed.
⏱️ Time your abs: Your 10-min weighted ab block stays as a circuit. Going heavier is better than going longer — 25–30 lb crunches beat 20 lb for longer.
😴 Sleep & eat: Hypertrophy requires a small surplus. Aim for 0.8–1g protein per lb of bodyweight and 7–9 hrs sleep. Without these, lifts go up but muscle doesn't.
🦵 Leg day targets: Month 1 → 125–130 lb squat. Month 2 → 140–150 lb. Month 3 → 155–175 lb. Adjust if needed — form > weight.
